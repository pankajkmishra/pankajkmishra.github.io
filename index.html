I’m Pankaj K Mishra (पंकज मिश्रा), a Senior Scientist at the Geological Survey of Finland (GTK). My research interests include magnetotelluric geophysics, inverse theory, uncertainty quantification, scientific machine learning, and multiphysics data integration. Currently, I am working on (1) integrating multi-source geophysical and geoscientific data into inversion algorithms, (2) making uncertainty quantification mainstream in geophysical inversion, and (3) using scientific machine learning to speed up forward solvers. Feel free to explore my projects, publications, and research interests, or get in touch [] to discuss potential collaborations.
